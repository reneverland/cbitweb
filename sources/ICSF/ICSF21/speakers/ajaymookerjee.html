Invited Speaker

Dr. Ajay MOOKERJEE

Dr. Mookerjee is the founding director of Fair Square Financial, and as Senior Advisor to Warburg Pincus, he is co-investor and board member of Max-Leumi Cards, driving both companies continued growth. Over the prior 22 years, he was corporate division and region head at CapOne and Suntrust, building billion-dollar credit card businesses and at Booz.Allen/Digital Credit, where seconding as managing advisor, he built billion-dollar bancassurance and sales finance businesses for AIG, GE and enduring products/platforms for Thai Farmers Bank, Fannie Mae, IBM, Cisco and D&B.

Dr. Mookerjee served in India as board-member and advisor to JM Financial, KPMG, Zephyr Capital and TVS Capital and was the President of the HBS Research Center. He has served on New Jersey governor Murphy’s economic transition team.

Dr. Mookerjee holds a Bachelor of Technology from the IIT-Kanpur and a Doctorate from the Harvard Business School, researching E-Finance Marketplace Structures, co-authoring the books Global Electronic Banking and Business Solutions for the Global Poor and is considered a thought leader in the fintech industry. 

Speech Title: Fintech and Bank Strategies for the post-CBDC era

Abstract: Online, AI, and reusable software technologies enabled fintechs gain a small niche in banking, challenging banks with innovative point solutions three-fold better in cost and service. With blockchain, they are enabling central bank digital currencies with unique identities, transaction traceability, and speed, changing the very concept of money and banking, taking back deposits from banks, eliminating bank runs and over-exposed credit markets. Banks must adapt to survive, but their revenues may shrink. Fintechs are preparing for the new reorder and could rapidly grab a significant share and valuation of the banking market.
