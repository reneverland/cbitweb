Speech Title: Fintech Strategies for Market Penetration: Specialization Versus Generalization

Abstract: We discuss market penetration strategies for Fintech firms. Fintechs generate both first and second order efficiency, but firms with more diverse product lines are economically more secure. A loose federation, using ideas from Cooperative Game Theory, might emerge as the future organizational form of choice in the Financial world.
