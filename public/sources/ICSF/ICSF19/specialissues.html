Financial innovation offers a global forum for exchanging innovative research findings across all fields of financial research. Areas of interest include asset pricing, derivative pricing and hedging, high-frequency and algorithmic trading, disruptive models and more.

IEEE Access

IEEE Access is a multidisciplinary, applications-oriented, all-electronic archival journal that continuously presents the results of original research or development across all of IEEE's fields of interest. Supported by author publication fees, its hallmarks are a rapid peer review and publication process with open access to all readers.

Journal of Systems Science and Information (JSSI)

Journal of Systems Science and Information (JSSI) (ISSN 1478-9906, CN10-1192/N) is bimonthly, a peer-reviewed multi-disciplinary journal that focuses on systems science and systems engineering, management science, and information science. This journal aims to provide a platform for sharing new developments in the research fields of systems science and management science and promotes applications of these cutting-edge theories and methods to real world. Topics covered by this journal include but not limited to the following list: Systems Science and Systems Engineering, Management Science, Economics, Finance, Modeling Technique, Forecasting Science, Information Science and Information Technology. This journal is sponsored by Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science of Chinese Academy of Sciences and edited by Systems Engineering Society of China and Science Press.
