Invited Speaker

Dr. Eric Zheng

Eric Zheng is the Ashbel Smith Professor of Information Systems with a joint appointment in Finance at the Jindal School of Management, University of Texas at Dallas. He received his PhD from the Wharton School of Business, University of Pennsylvania.

His recent research interests focus on Fintech and Blockchain. He currently serves as a senior editor for Information Systems Research and is the co-editor for its special issue on Fintech and Blockchain. He co-founded the premier Informs Workshop on Data Science.
