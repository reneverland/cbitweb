Electronic Commerce Research

Electronic Commerce Research serves as a catalyst for new research and a forum for disseminating the latest findings in all facets of electronic commerce. The journal’s broad scope encompasses core enabling technologies as well as the implications of these technologies for societies, economies, businesses, and individuals. Readers will find a host of important theoretical and empirical research findings that are leading the way to a better understanding of electronic commerce and its impact.

A sampling of topics as they relate to the internet and electronic commerce include intelligent agents technologies and their impact; economics of electronic commerce; virtual electronic commerce systems; service creation and provisioning; supply chain management through the internet; collaborative learning, gaming, and work; and workflow for electronic commerce applications.

In addition to its regular issues, the journal publishes periodic issues devoted to a single subject area.

Financial Innovation

This journal offers a global forum for exchanging innovative research findings across all fields of financial research. Areas of interest include asset pricing, derivative pricing and hedging, high-frequency and algorithmic trading, disruptive models and more.

Journal of Systems Science & Information

Journal of Systems Science & Information is a peer-reviewed multi-disciplinary journal that focuses on systems science, management science and information science. The journal aims to provide a platform for sharing new developments in the research fields of systems science and information science and promotes applications of these cutting-edge theories and methods to real worlds. The editors welcome original and unpublished contributions in all areas of systems science and systems engineering, management science, economics, finance, modeling technique, forecasting science, information science and information technology, and operation research. Articles addressed on the innovative achievements in solving the practical problems and review of important research developments are also welcomed.

Journal of Systems Science & Information aims to be a leading academic journal in the fields of systems science and information science. It seeks to provide original contributions that add to the understanding of complex systems from the perspectives of systems science, management science and information science. The journal should be of interest to a broad interdisciplinary readers including academics, practitioners, business managers and consultants.
