All submissions must have not been accepted or conditionally accepted by a journal.

  • Submissions should be made via the EasyChair System.
  • Both abstract and paper submissions are welcome. Abstract submissions should be 2-3 pages. Paper submissions should be 3-5 pages, including abstract, main text, figures, tables, and references.
  • Submissions exceeding 5 pages will be desk rejected.

Important Dates:

Journal publication opportunities

Financial Innovation

FIN emphasizes emerging financial products, processes and services that are enabled by the introduction of disruptive technologies. FIN is peer-reviewed and publishes both high-quality academic (theoretical or empirical) and practical papers in the broad ranges of financial innovation. Financial Innovation ranked 8th in finance journals according to impact factor (Latest IF=6.793).

 

IET Blockchain

IET Blockchain aims to publish high-quality research papers focusing on the latest advances in blockchain and its applications. The journal reports fundamental research results, cutting-edge technologies, latest developments and emerging applications of the blockchain technology.
